# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and
harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- being respectful and constructive
- accepting feedback gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- trolling or insulting comments
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of
Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other
contributions that do not align with this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project
maintainers through private channels.
